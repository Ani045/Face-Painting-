Our Colorful Story

Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate artists, AussieFaces has grown to become Australia's premier face painting service, bringing joy and creativity to events across the country.

Our team of professional artists uses only the highest-quality, skin-safe products to ensure beautiful results and happy customers of all ages.

From Sydney to Perth, Brisbane to Melbourne, our talented artists have delighted thousands of clients at parties, corporate events, and festivals throughout Australia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our face painting services

Is the face paint safe for sensitive skin?

Absolutely! We use only professional, water-based face paints that are FDA-approved, hypoallergenic, and designed specifically for use on skin. They're easily removed with soap and water.

How many faces can you paint per hour?

Each artist can paint approximately 10-15 faces per hour, depending on the complexity of the designs. For larger events, we recommend booking multiple artists.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially for weekend events during peak season (October-December). For large corporate events, 2-3 months notice is preferred.

What areas of Australia do you service?

We have artists based in all major cities including Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, Canberra, and Hobart. We can also travel to regional areas for an additional travel fee.
